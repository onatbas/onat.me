Lunch Roulette

Working in a huge company? Don't get to meet a lot of people?
Schedule lunchs with random people from your team! Great way for meeting new people, and fun!

How It Works?

That's easy! First, you log in.

And then you create a roulette! Use /createnewroulette command for this.

Choose how you want to schedule it, and you're done!

And join! Also let your colleagues join!

Before 3 hours to actual event, winners will be announced!

Schedule bunch of these as a catalog for the team, make it a regular thing!
